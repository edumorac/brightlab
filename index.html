Our Story

We are an educational game company that creates innovative products designed with learning in mind. We are passionate about games and storytelling and we work everyday to combine the best of those worlds to create awesome learning experiences for children.

We believe that education should be an interactive and empowering experience for all people involved. Which is why we design products that allow users to be creative and to tell their own stories.

Our Mission


Our goal is to disrupt the educational world with games, storytelling, and technology. We are striving to develop games and content that encourage children to be autonomous and creative.

We are working to design games and stories that allow children to be creative, express themselves with independently, and connect with their environment through exploration.

Our Approach


At Kidobit, autonomy is fundamental. We believe that true learning happens when learners are the protagonists of their own learning experience.

Our products are designed to provide an opportunity for kids to build their own knowledge, explore, and have fun.
